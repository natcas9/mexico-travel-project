Why Mexico City?

Mexico the city of Palaces

Mainly known by its amazing food, and its beautiful architecture. Mexico City is one of the biggest cities in the world. This city never sleeps and is full of traditions and history. Considered as the city pf palaces because of the mansions and palaces in Mexico's City downtown. This is a place you must visit at least once in your life.
